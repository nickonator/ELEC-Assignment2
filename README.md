# SES Volunteer Coordination App
A prototype app for coordinating State Emergency Service (SES) volunteers 
during emergencies such as floods and missing persons incidents.

## Why It Exists
Field emergencies are high-stress and time-sensitive. This app gives team 
leaders and volunteers a single, low-cognitive-load platform to coordinate 
without switching between tools.

**Built for:** SES volunteers and team leaders operating during state-wide emergencies.

## Key Features
- Login system for both volunteers and team leaders
- Volunteers can set their weekly availability
- Volunteers can view, accept, or decline assigned tasks
- Volunteers can receive direct messages from team leaders
- Team leaders can create incidents and assign them to volunteers
- Team leaders can send direct notifications to volunteers

## Quick Start

### Login Credentials
| Role | Username | Password |
|------|----------|----------|
| Volunteer | user1@gmail.com | abc123 |
| Volunteer | user2@gmail.com | def456 |
| Team Leader | leader@gmail.com | leader123 |

### As a Volunteer
1. **Set availability** - Click "Home" or "Availability" in the nav bar, 
   click squares to mark availability using the key, then click "Save Schedule"
2. **View assignments** - Click "Assignments" in the nav bar, click any 
   assignment to see details. Accept or Decline pending assignments using 
   the buttons in the Incident Summary.
3. **View notifications** - Click "Notifications" in the nav bar, click 
   any message to expand it in the Notification Summary.

### As a Team Leader
1. **Create an incident** - Fill in Incident Type, Address, and Additional 
   Info, select a volunteer from the dropdown, then click "Assign Volunteer"
2. **Send a notification** - After creating a task you'll be redirected 
   automatically. Select a user, write a message, and click "Send Notification"
3. **Go back to login** - Click the blue left-arrow on any screen

## Common Confusion
- **Profile button does nothing** - it's a placeholder in this prototype
- **Home and Availability go to the same page** - both link to 
  "Update My Availability" in this prototype version
